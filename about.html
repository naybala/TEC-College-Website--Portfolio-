About Us

Our mission is to provide outstanding education, advance knowledge and benefit the world.

Our vision is that, the University of Chichester will be internationally recognised as a beacon of good practice for high quality, student-centred higher education within a supportive community of learning.

Who We Are

We believe our university is enriched by having students and staff from a wide variety of backgrounds.

Higher education should be open to everyone who has the ability and potential to benefit from it, regardless of age, ethnicity, disability or family background.

We know from our experience that a diverse student group creates a lively and successful community.
